Hello everyone. I would like to begin by introducing myself.

I am a single brother with no children of my own yet. I am from Mahikeng, which is a small town situated in the North West province of South Africa. The weather in our town is quite unpredictable as it can change from hot to cold or sunny to cloudy on any given day.

I have done a bit of travelling and have been able to live in parts of South Africa and even across the border in Botswana before returning to my home town.

I have been a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints for 18 years now. I also had the privilege of serving a full-time mission in the South Africa Cape Town Mission from September 2008 to October 2010. The things that I love about the church are its teachings about Jesus Christ, the holy scriptures, temples, opportunities to serve and the BYU-Pathway program.

I currently work in sales in the insurance industry and it is quite a challenging job. I have decided to enrol for the Web and Computer Programming certificate to learn more about the design of websites and to give myself better job prospects in the future. I am currently enjoying learning new things about the design of websites.

I am looking forward to getting to know all of my new classmates and learning together. Thank you all for making time to have a look at my webpage.
